Family Life

In 1814, Charles Babbage married Georgiana Whitmore at St. Michael's Church in Teignmouth, Devon. His father, for some reason, never gave his approvation. They lived in tranquility at 5 Devonshire Street, Portland Place, London. Only Three of their 8 children became adult. Tragically, Charles' father, his wife and one of his sons all died in 1827.

Children:

  1. Benjamin Herschel Babbage (1815)
  2. Charles Whitmore Babbage (1817)
  3. Georgiana Whitmore Babbage (1818)
  4. Edward Stewart Babbage (1819)
  5. Dugald Bromheald Babbage (1823)
  6. Henry Prevost Babbage (1824)
  7. Alexander Forbes Babbage (1827)
  8. Timothy grant Babbage (1829)
